STOP ASIAN HATE

Credit: Actor and activist William Lex Ham addresses a crowd on Boston Common during a rally against anti-Asian hate. (Quincy Walters/WBUR) https://www.wbur.org/news/2021/03/14/stop-asian-hate-protest-boston-common-coronavirus

Asian hate is becoming a more and more serious issue nowadays since the pandemic occurred. A lot of people were using it as an excuse to attack Asian/ Chinese people. Based on The compilation of hate crime data from the Center for the Study of Hate and Extremism, indicated that the anti-Asian hate crime rate increased by 339% in 2020 compared to years before in the U.S. Also, according to the research, "New York City had a particularly drastic rise, from 30 to 133 anti-Asian hate crimes, a 343 percent increase. San Francisco also experienced an alarming jump, from 9 to 60 crimes, a 567 percent increase. And Los Angeles had a similarly sizable hike of 173 percent." Asian people are living under the threaten nowadays, that's why we have to stand out for all Asians to stop Asian hate and protect ourselves.
